# News Research — April 2026

## The 2026 Strait of Hormuz Crisis (ONGOING)

Following joint US-Israel military strikes on Iran on Feb 28, 2026 (including the killing of Khamenei), the IRGC has effectively blockaded the Strait of Hormuz.

### Key Events
- **Mar 1**: First-ever state-led explosive drone boat (USV) attack on commercial shipping — MV MKD VYOM hit in Gulf of Oman
- **Mar 2**: Iran announces it will attack any ship trying to pass through the strait
- **Mar 7**: IRGC hits two oil tankers with drones (MT Prima, MV Louis P)
- **Mar 8**: Brent crude surpasses $100/barrel for first time in 4 years
- **Mar 11**: Large wave of attacks — at least 3 vessels damaged, 20 crew rescued, 3 missing. US intelligence reports Iran has begun planting naval mines in the strait. US sinks 16 Iranian minelayers.
- **Mar 11**: IRGC declares "not one litre of oil" will pass through
- **Mar 12**: 21 confirmed attacks on merchant ships. Brent peaks at $126/barrel
- **Mar 27**: IRGC creates a TOLLBOOTH — charging ships for "safe passage"
- **Late Mar**: Houthis resume Red Sea attacks, claim first missile launch at Israel since Oct 2025 ceasefire
- **US Operation Epic Fury**: US destroys 17 Iranian warships and one submarine

### IRGC Tactics Documented
- **Fast boat swarms**: 10-20 boats swarming a single ship from 360°, speeds of 50-70 knots
- **Naval mines**: Deployed from small vessels and submersibles, concealed in tunnels/caves along coast
- **Drone swarms**: Shahed-style cheap drones ($20k each) overwhelming multi-million dollar interceptor systems — "saturation attacks"
- **Explosive drone boats (USVs)**: Waterborne kamikaze drones, first confirmed state use Mar 2026
- **Helicopter boarding**: Commandos rappel from helicopters onto tankers (Nov-Dec 2025 seizures, Qeshm Island area)
- **Layered "magazine-drain" doctrine**: Mines + submarines + coastal missiles + fast boat swarms + drone swarms combined to exhaust defensive ammunition
- **Mini-submarines**: Ghadir-class, shallow water capable, torpedo and mine-laying
- **Toll checkpoint**: IRGC literally charging ships to pass through

### Coalition Tech Responses
- **HELIOS Laser**: USS Preble successfully destroyed 4 drones with 60-120kW directed energy laser. Integrates with Aegis. Cost per shot = electricity only vs million-dollar interceptors
- **Songbow Project**: $30M contract for 400kW pulsed laser system (June 2025)
- **LUCAS drones**: US deploying its own Iranian-derived strike drones

### Houthi Red Sea (Resumed)
- 100+ attacks from Nov 2023-Oct 2025
- Paused during Gaza ceasefire Oct 2025
- Resumed Feb 28, 2026 in response to Iran war
- Weapons: UAVs, USVs, UUVs (underwater drones), ballistic/cruise missiles, limpet mines, small arms from boats
